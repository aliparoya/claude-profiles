## Aesthetic principles

A few directions that hold even before the palette and type are pinned down:

- Prefer restraint over decoration. The content carries the page.
- Let structure show through. Whitespace, hierarchy, and alignment do most of the work.
- One accent at a time. If everything competes for attention, nothing has it.
- Materials over ornament. Real surfaces, real tools, real workspaces beat abstract gradients.
